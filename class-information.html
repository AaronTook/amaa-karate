Rules of the Do Jang

  1. Safety of our students is the number one priority!

  2. Always bow when entering and leaving the Do Jang.

  3. Gum, candy, and other food are not allowed in the Do Jang.

  4. Shoes should not be worn in the training area (except martial arts training shoes).

  5. Uniforms should be kept clean, neat, and orderly at all times. If desired, a solid white or black T-shirt may be worn under the uniform.

  6. Jewelry should not be worn while training or testing (except wedding bands or religious items).

  7. Respect others at all times. All martial artists, especially those of higher rank, should be addressed as "sir" or "ma'am".

  8. Class is scheduled to begin and end promptly at the announced times. Please be on time, prepared, and ready to go.

Registration

All new students may attend their first class for FREE with no obligation!

Students wishing to register for classes should print the following forms, fill them out and and bring them to class to turn in. Our Registration Form must be completed by each student/parent during each session they train with us. The Concussion Form must also be competed by students/parents once per year. This form confirms your awareness that AMAA Instructors are certified in concussion prevention and recognition for youth sports and activities to the standards set by the states of Michigan and Ohio. This form is available at the registration table the night of class. Students in some of our schools may also compete their registration online.
Please contact us with any questions about the registration process.
