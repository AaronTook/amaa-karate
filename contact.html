Frequently Asked questions

Where are we located?

We have schools in Southwest Michigan and Southeast Ohio. Our schools are located in Lake Orion Michigan, Clarkston Michigan, and Englewood Ohio.

Where can I register for classes?

You can register either in person the first night of class or you can print and fill out the registration forms and bring them to class.

How old do I have to be to take classes?

Students ages 4-5 can register for our Little Ninjas classes at our Clarkston and Lake Orion schools. Anyone ages 6 and up can sign up for our beginner classes.

Where can I get help with my requirements?

You can find videos of our forms on our Youtube Channel. If you have questions about other requirements, ask your instructor during class.
